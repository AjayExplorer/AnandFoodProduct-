Our Story

Anand Foods, established in 2004, is a leading distributor known for its commitment to quality and variety in the Indian snack foods sector, specializing in banana chips, assorted mixture items, and pakkavada, among other popular snacks.

For over two decades, we have been committed to preserving traditional recipes while maintaining the highest standards of quality and taste. Our journey started in a small kitchen with a passion for creating snacks that remind people of home.
